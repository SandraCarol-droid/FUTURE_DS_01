Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from the Superstore dataset to uncover business insights and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis was conducted using Microsoft Power BI after cleaning and organizing the raw sales dataset.

The main objective was to answer key business questions such as:

Which products generate the most revenue?
How do sales change over time?
Which categories or regions are most profitable?
Where should the business focus to grow faster?

The final deliverable includes an interactive dashboard and business recommendations based on the findings.

Project Tasks
Data Cleaning & Preparation

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

Removed inconsistencies and checked for missing values
Formatted date columns properly
Verified revenue and profit calculations
Organized categorical fields for analysis
Business Questions Answered
1. Which Products Generate the Most Revenue?

The analysis showed that the Cisco TelePresence System EX90 Videoconferencing Unit generated the highest revenue.

Top-performing sub-categories included:

Phones
Machines
Chairs
Tables
2. How Do Sales Change Over Time?

Revenue trends over time revealed:

Sales generally increased over the years
Certain months experienced stronger performance peaks
Seasonal fluctuations affected overall revenue patterns

The dashboard includes a time-series visualization to monitor these changes.

3. Which Categories or Regions Are Most Profitable?
High-Performing Categories

The most profitable product categories/sub-categories were:

Phones
Chairs
Storage
Machines
Regional Performance

The analysis compared performance across:

Central
East
South
West

The central region showed strong profitability and sales contribution.

4. Business Growth Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the business should focus on:

Expanding high-performing product categories like Phones and Chairs
Increasing marketing efforts in profitable regions
Improving inventory planning around seasonal demand trends
Promoting top-selling products with strong revenue performance
